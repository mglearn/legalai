Tools and requirements

Pick one free AI chatbot

Claude, ChatGPT, Z.ai, or Mistral all have free tiers that work for every activity today. If you have none set up, use the BoodleBox code from the session. We also look at Claude for Legal as an example of purpose-built, review-gated legal AI.

The one rule before you type: never put confidential, privileged, or client-identifying facts into a consumer chatbot. Every scenario here is fictional for that reason.
